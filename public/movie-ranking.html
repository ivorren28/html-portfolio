My top 3 movies of all times

John wick

Wick is a legendary hitman who is reluctantly drawn back into the criminal underworld after retiring.

Extraction

The extraction genre is all about engaging in a high-stakes environment where the goal is not just survival, but the pursuit of acquiring and extracting valuable loot, all while balancing the risk of potentially losing it all

Equalizer

A former special service commando who faked his own death in hopes of living out a quiet life. Instead, he comes out of his self-imposed retirement to save a young girl, and finds his desire for justice reawakened after coming face-to-face with members of a brutal Russian gang.
